About Me

        Hi Everyone, welcome to my page! I am currently a Senior Accountant at a large multi-national corporation in Broomfield, Colorado. I have a BA in Accounting from Dakota Wesleyan University and a Master of Business Administration from Oregon State University. I have worked in a variety of environments; small startups with limited resources, large corporations going through mergers, and sizeable established companies. I have worked at the corporate headquarters, small regional offices and even in the maintenance offices of a papermill.

        My favorite roles have elements in both accounting and analytics. I perform the monthly close process regularly and have reconciled countless balance sheets. I have experience forecasting, performing variance analysis, budgeting, calculating ROI, and performing other analytics related to capital expenditure requests. In many of my roles, I have found myself constricted by the limited data mining/BI resources available to people in my position. Most of the time IT has to be involved, and their schedules are already too packed to add an accounting project. I have dabbled with data mining and VBA in the past but only began to scratch the surface of what tools are out there. I have been wanting to dive deeper for years and am excited for the opportunity to learn a new, fun art that will compliment my accounting skills.
